Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical recovery timeline for breast reduction surgery?

Most patients return to work within 2-3 weeks. Full recovery typically takes 6-8 weeks, with final results visible after 3-6 months. We provide comprehensive post-operative care and guidance throughout your recovery journey.

Will my insurance cover breast reduction surgery?

Many insurance providers cover breast reduction when it's deemed medically necessary. Our team will help you navigate the insurance process and document any physical symptoms to support your case.

How long do the results of breast reduction surgery last?

Results are typically permanent, though natural aging and weight fluctuations can affect breast size over time. Maintaining a stable weight helps ensure long-lasting results.

What kind of scarring can I expect?

While some scarring is inevitable, we use advanced techniques to minimize visibility. Scars typically fade significantly over time, and we provide detailed scar care instructions for optimal healing.

How do I know if I'm a good candidate for breast reduction?

Ideal candidates are in good overall health, experience physical discomfort from large breasts, and have realistic expectations. During your consultation, we'll conduct a thorough evaluation to determine your suitability.
