Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recovery time for breast reduction surgery?

Most patients return to work within 2-3 weeks, with full recovery taking 6-8 weeks. Each patient's recovery journey is unique.

Will the results be permanent?

Yes, breast reduction results are typically permanent, though natural aging and weight changes can affect breast size over time.

Is breast reduction covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by provider and circumstance. We can help you determine your eligibility during consultation.
